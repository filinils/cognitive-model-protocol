# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Participants in the CMP project are expected to contribute respectfully, thoughtfully, and in good faith.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of different experiences and perspectives.
- Critique ideas without attacking people.
- Assume that draft language may be incomplete and improve it constructively.
- Raise ethical concerns clearly and seriously.
- Make room for disagreement, especially on scope and misuse risks.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Dismissive or demeaning language.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Using project spaces for spam, advertising, or unrelated promotion.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may moderate issues, pull requests, and discussions to preserve a respectful project environment. Repeated or severe violations may result in removal from project spaces.
